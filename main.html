Determining The Size of an Atom

The objective of this lab is for students to apply their knowledge in the fields of geometry and chemistry, and form connections between the two fields. A set of instructions are given, as well as multiple instances of the GeoGebra 3D Calculator. Using the main lab window, students are expected to utilize the given tools to complete the lab.
