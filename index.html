Determining The Size of an Atom

This is a lab designed to allow students to apply their geometric and chemistry knowledge. They will be assigned a set of instructions and a 3D Geogebra Calculator. Through this, they will utilize these tools to successfully complete the Scratch project.

About

Shown is a photo of Mr. Bangs working
on a lab project. Unfortunately due to
the COVID-19 pandemic, students are unable
to work in the lab, preventing them from
completing hands-on assignments. Therefore,
Mr. Bangs had the idea to assign his Zero
Period students to create an interactive program,
allowing them to virtually obtain the same
experience.
